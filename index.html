01 · About

Who I Am

I'm a Java Developer with 3+ years of experience building backend systems for enterprise banking and fintech clients. My work lives at the intersection of distributed services, workflow orchestration, and system integration — where business logic is complex, data flows are non-trivial, and reliability is non-negotiable.

What drew me to backend development — and keeps me here — is the mechanics of it. How JPA and Hibernate abstract an entire relational database into clean, maintainable code. How distributed microservices coordinate across the network without tight coupling. How a well-designed Camunda workflow can take a fragmented, manual process and make it predictable, auditable, and fully automated.

I work primarily with Java 21, Spring Boot, PostgreSQL, Flyway, and Camunda 7 & 8, usually in microservices environments. I'm currently looking for Java/Spring Boot backend roles where I can keep building at this depth.
